Mathematical Model

The equations driving the HYDRO-STORM engine.

Starling Equation

SV = MaxSV * (1 - e^(-k * Preload))

Stroke Volume increases with Preload (Volume) exponentially, but plateaus. The 'k' constant is determined by Contractility (EF). Weak hearts plateau much earlier (Fluid Intolerance).

Congestion Model

Edema = Preload > 18 ? (Preload - 18)^1.8 : 0

Once hydrostatic pressure (Preload) exceeds oncotic pressure (~18mmHg), fluid transudes into the alveoli exponentially. This is the "cliffs edge" of fluid resuscitation.

ANDROMEDA-SHOCK (2019)

FEAST Trial (2011)
